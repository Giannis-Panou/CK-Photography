Welcome to CK Photography

Hi, I am CK, a passionate photographer with a love for capturing life's most beautiful and fleeting moments. Based in Thessaloniki, I specialize in portrait and landscape photography, offering a unique blend of artistic vision and technical expertise to deliver stunning images that tell a story.

My Journey

Photography has always been more than just a profession for me—it's a way of seeing the world. My journey began 20 years ago, when I picked up my first camera and discovered the magic of preserving memories. Since then, I've dedicated myself to honing my craft, learning from every shot, and continuously exploring new techniques to push the boundaries of what's possible.

Services Offered

  • Portrait Photography: Capturing the true essence of individuals and families in natural, comfortable settings.
  • Wedding Photography: Documenting the magic of your special day with a blend of candid and posed shots.
  • Commercial Photography: Creating professional images that enhance your brand and engage your audience.
  • Event Photography: Ensuring every detail and emotion of your event is captured for lasting memories.
  • Landscape Photography: Bringing the beauty of nature to life with stunning scenery shots.
